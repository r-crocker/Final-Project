My School

The University of New Haven, founded on the Yale Campus in 1920, is private, coeducational university that has been recognized by The Princeton Review and U.S. News & World Report for academic excellence. Located between New York City and Boston in the shoreline city of West Haven, the University is a diverse and vibrant community of more than 7,000 students from across the globe.

Fun Stuff

In the area there are several options for cultural activities. In downtown New Haven there are several museums and theaters including the Yale Art Gallery, the Center for British Art, the Yale Peabody Museum, the Shubert Theater, the Long Wharf Theater, and the Yale Repertory Theater. Between the UNH campus and Downtown New Haven, there are three food truck hubs which are filled with delicious foods from different culture.
